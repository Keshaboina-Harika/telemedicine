Shirodhara Ayurveda Treatment

Shirodhara is a unique Ayurvedic remedy that involves a gentle and steady sluice of warm oil painting or herbal liquids poured over the forepart, also known as the" Third Eye," in a nonstop sluice for a period of time. Procedure of Shirodhara Ayurveda Treatment

The Shirodhara Ayurveda treatment procedure is a simple yet effective system of relaxation that involves the following way: Consultation Before the remedy, the Ayurvedic guru will have a discussion with the case to determine their dosha( body type) and to bandy any beginning health conditions. Preparation The case is made to lie down on a massage table, and a cloth or kerchief is placed over their forepart to help oil painting from entering the eyes. The case is also asked to relax and breathe deeply. Selection of oil painting The Ayurvedic guru selects an oil painting or herbal liquid that's suitable for the case's dosha and health condition. The oil painting is warmed to a comfortable temperature. Duration: The remedy generally lasts between 30 to 60 twinkles, depending on the case's condition. Conclusion: After the remedy is complete, the case is allowed to rest for a many twinkles before getting up sluggishly. The redundant oil painting is wiped off, and the case is given a warm shower.
